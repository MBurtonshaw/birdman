Bird is the Word

Harvey Raymond Randall Birdman attorney at law, is a highly qualified professional. And he has a pet falcon or something. He has won many high profile cases, which you may see the details of below. If you have an interest in Mr. Birdman representing you in an upcoming case, please contact us.

Successful Cases

Apache Chief v Javalux

After an incident with a hot cup of coffee, Apache Chief, member of the Super Friends organization, finds himself unable to use his power of enlargement. He sought to sue the Javalux coffee corporation, but in the end the case was dropped. Technically, a victory.

Mystery Inc. Case

Shaggy and Scooby Doo of Mystery Inc. were on their way to solve a monster case when they were pulled over and falsely arrested for driving under the influence. After the delivery of important evidence by Velma Dinkley on the stand, and the actual capture of the "monster", the defendants were found innocent.

Speed Buggy v California

Mr. Speed Buggy was found driving recklessly on the interstate which turned into a high speed chase, and Mr. Buggy attempting to jump the median in the road. In court, it was determined that Mark and Debbie, two acquaintances of Mr. Buggy, were sitting on the sentient car's remote control.

The Case of Fred Flintstone

Mr. Fred Flintstone was falsely accused and arrested for allegedly heading a mob organization. Harvey was able to demonstrate that the mafia don is just another persona of Fred's. After having been hit on the head with a bowling ball so many times in his life, Fred was proven to have a multiple personality disorder.

Mr. Race Bannon v Dr. Benton Quest

A custody battle over two boys between Dr. Benton Quest, and Mr. Race Bannon, Dr. Quest's hired bodyguard. Mr. Bannon felt that he had raised the boys throughout their life and sought custody over Dr. Quest. In the end, it turns out Mr. Bannon was a robot controlled by the evil Dr. Zin.
